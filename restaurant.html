RESTAURANTS

Restaurant of The Month

9 Conduit St, Mayfair, London W1S 2XG

Sketch

The afternoon tea is scrumptious! The decor here does not fail to impress and you should try to visit the other rooms as well as the famous bathroom. The service was a bit slow unfortunately and the tea was slightly pricey - but definitely worth it in the end!

Rating: 4.6/5

16-22 Great Russell St, Fitzrovia, London WC1B 3NN

Dalloway Terrace

The decor, the warm welcome, the great menu - some of the things that make Dalloway Terrace a great restaurant. You can look forward to the different themes every season and the changing menu too. Sadly the service was slightly rushed and the place felt crowded.

Rating: 4.6/5

3 Three Quays Walk, Lower Thames St, Tower, London EC3R 6AH

Coppa Club

Don't forget to book the igloos if you want to try out this restaurant (you might share the igloo with others if you are with up to 4 people)! Whilst the views (we recommend going either during sunset or at night) are spectacular, the food was very very average.

Rating: 3.8/5

34 Grosvenor Square, S Audley St, Mayfair, London W1K 2HD

34 Mayfair

We visited during Christmas and the festive decor was stunning. The normal flower decorations make it perfect for a Valentine's day event too! If you don't want a whole meal (pricey for what you get), we recommend going just for a drink and emjoying the atmosphere.

Rating: 4.1/5

8 Charlotte St, Fitzrovia, London W1T 2LS

Norma

Norma offers a contemporary Silician menu in a Moorish-style setting. The service here was excellent - friendly, attentive and efficient. The small plates and the pasta were tasty but we felt that there was nothing special about the food overall.

Rating: 4.3/5

14th floor, 120 Fenchurch St, Langbourn, London EC3M 5BA

14 Hills

The view of London from 14 Hills is breathtaking and we enjoyed watching the sunset at the bar. The food took a long time to arrive and ended up being lukewarm. Also, the restaurant was quite noisy so we would probably not recommend this restaurant for a romantic dinner.

Rating: 4.2/5
